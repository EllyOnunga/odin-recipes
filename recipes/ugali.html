Classic Ugali

Home

Ugali is a staple East African dish made from maize flour and water. It's simple, filling, and best enjoyed with vegetables, meat, or beans

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a pot
  2. Slowly add maize flour while stirring continuously to avoid lumps
  3. Keep stirring until the mixture thickens and starts pulling away from the sides of the pot
  4. Cover and let cook for 3-5 more minutes on low heat
  5. Stir again, then turn off the heat. Shape into a round mound and serve warm
