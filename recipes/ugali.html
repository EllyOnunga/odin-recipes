Abasic but essential recipe for making fluffy, perfect cooked steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tea spoonful salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice in cold water untill the water runs clear
  2. In a saucepan, combine rice, water, and salt
  3. Bring to a boil over medium heat
  4. Once boiling, reduce heat to low, cover with a lid, and simmer for 15- 18 minutes
  5. Turn off heat and let sit for 5 mins. Fluff with a fork and serve
