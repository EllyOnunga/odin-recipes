Tasty Stewed Beans

Home

Enjoy a hearty and protein rich dish made from slow-cooked beans with vegetables and spices

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dry beans
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 2 diced tomatoes
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tsp cooking oil
  • Any spices available

Steps

  1. Soak the beans overnight or for atleast 6 hours
  2. Boil beans in fresh water until soft, about 45 - 60 mins
  3. In a pan, heat oil and saute onions, garlic, tomatoes, and green pepper
  4. Add cooked beans and stir well, let it simmer for for 15 - 20 mins
  5. Add salt and spices to taste. Stir and serve hot
